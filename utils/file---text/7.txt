catalysts. This work has shown that Re can promote the
epoxidationofethylenewithoutthepresenceofCl,althougha
sequential impregnation method must be employed instead of
the co-impregnation technique often cited in the patent
literature. Re only promotes EO selectivity at very small
loadings, with a large decrease in catalyst activity seen as
loadings are increased above 50ppm. The selectivity trends
